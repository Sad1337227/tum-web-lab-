Accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy.

Tesla, Inc. is a trailblazing leader in the electric vehicle and clean energy industries, renowned for its groundbreaking innovations and commitment to sustainability. Since its inception, Tesla has revolutionized the automotive world with its cutting-edge technology and visionary approach.

At Tesla, we believe in the power of clean energy to transform the future. Our electric vehicles, from the high-performance Model S to the versatile Model 3, are designed to deliver unparalleled efficiency and driving experience while reducing the environmental impact. Our mission extends beyond cars; with our Solar Roof, Powerwall, and other energy solutions, we strive to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

Innovation is at the heart of everything we do at Tesla. Our commitment to pushing the boundaries of technology is evident in our continual advancements in battery technology, autonomous driving capabilities, and renewable energy solutions. We are driven by the vision of a sustainable future where transportation and energy are clean, efficient, and accessible to all.

Tesla's approach is not just about creating products; it's about creating a movement. We aim to inspire and empower individuals and communities to embrace sustainable practices. Our global network of Superchargers, Gigafactories, and service centers is designed to support our growing customer base and ensure that the transition to electric vehicles and clean energy is seamless and convenient.

Through relentless innovation, a commitment to sustainability, and a vision for a cleaner future, Tesla continues to lead the charge in revolutionizing the automotive and energy sectors. Join us as we drive the world towards a brighter, greener tomorrow.

Model 3

The Tesla Model 3 is an affordable, high-efficiency electric vehicle designed for everyday use. Offering long range, fast acceleration, and advanced safety features, the Model 3 makes electric driving accessible to a broader audience.

Powerwall

Tesla Powerwall is a home battery system that stores energy for backup power, self-consumption, and off-grid use. It integrates seamlessly with solar energy systems, ensuring you have reliable and sustainable energy whenever you need it.

Solar Roof

Tesla's Solar Roof combines beautiful design with energy efficiency. These solar tiles integrate seamlessly with your home's architecture, providing clean energy while enhancing your home's aesthetic appeal.
